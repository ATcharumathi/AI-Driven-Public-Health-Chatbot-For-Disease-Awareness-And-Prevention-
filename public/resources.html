Trusted Resources & Policies

HealthBot provides information based on reliable, evidence-based, and verified sources. Below are the international and national health organizations, guidelines, and policies we refer to for disease awareness and prevention content.

🌍 Global Health Organizations

  • World Health Organization (WHO) – Global health policies, guidelines, and disease prevention strategies.
  • Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) – U.S.-based disease information, prevention tips, and vaccination guidelines.
  • UNICEF – Child health, maternal care, nutrition, and vaccination resources.
  • World Medical Association (WMA) – Global medical ethics and public health statements.

🇮🇳 National Health Policies (India)

  • National Health Portal of India (NHP) – Public health guidelines, diseases, and healthcare initiatives.
  • Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW) – National disease prevention policies, vaccination programs, and advisories.
  • Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) – Research-backed health information and COVID-19 resources.
  • AYUSH Ministry – Traditional medicine, yoga, and preventive healthcare approaches.

🦠 Disease-Specific Resources

  • WHO Coronavirus (COVID-19) Resources
  • WHO Global Malaria Programme
  • CDC Diabetes Information
  • CDC Hypertension Prevention
  • WHO Immunization Agenda 2030

📜 Ethical & Privacy Guidelines

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – Data privacy standards.
  • HIPAA (U.S. Health Privacy Law) – Protection of personal health information.
  • WMA Declaration of Helsinki – Ethical principles for medical information and research.
